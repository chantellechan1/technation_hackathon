About Us

Team UBC Engineers consists of two 4th year engineering students with very different specializations. See our bios below!

Catalina De Castro

Public Policy & Economics Analyst

In addition to Engineering, I study political science and commerce at UBC. As a queer first-generation immigrant and daughter of a business owner, I am passionate about finding robust technical solutions to address the intersectional needs of Canadian communities and businesses. As the public policy and economics analyst of the team, I saw this project as an opportunity to bridge the gap between what business owners need to survive this pandemic and what the government can provide.

Chantelle Chan

Full Stack Software Developer

I am an engineering student at UBC and an
LGBT+ asian-canadian software developer with 4 years of experience working on data analytics web applications. During my time at UBC, I have been heavily involved in various interdisciplinary engineering projects that apply data analytics in unexpected places. As the software developer of this team, I am excited to leverage my skills to create a tool that brings to light the most urgent needs of small business owners to the government bodies that aim to serve them.
